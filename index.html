About us

The flagship business fest of IIMK, Backwaters, creates a platform for students and academicians from leading institutes of the country to test their business acumen across all verticals of management. What started as a spark has today grown into being one of the most anticipated fests in the Indian B-School scenario. Bigger than any of its previous editions and with never before seen events, workshops and initiatives, Backwaters 2017 is all set to shake up the Indian B-world.
