“A leader is someone who demonstrates what is possible”

Backwaters presents to you Avatar the flagship invite-only leadership event of Backwaters. It is the ultimate challenge to select the one individual best suited to be the CEO. An Avatar is well equipped to overcome challenges of the corporate world, manage limited resources in the best way, come out of critical situations with strategic solutions combined with a never-ending pursuit for profitability, operational excellence and sustainability.Contestants will be put through a series of grueling events spanning over three days to test their true management potential.

So here is the perfect opportunity for you to demonstrate leadership your style.

Are you ready to battle it out till the end?
