SHIKSHA

03-Nov-2017

Shiksha is an attempt through which we aspire to make a difference in the lives of school and undergraduate students. Aimed at promoting higher education, Shiksha strives to create a unique learning experience by way of simulated IIM classroom learning, career counseling, a soft skills development session and much more. Recognizing the need for early career guidance, Shiksha has also planned a workshop for school students to help these children make informed career choices.
