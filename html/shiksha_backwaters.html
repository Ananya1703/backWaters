SHIKSHA

03-Nov-2017

DOWNLOAD

Shiksha is an attempt through which we aspire to make a difference in the lives of school and undergraduate students. Aimed at promoting higher education, Shiksha strives to create a unique learning experience by way of simulated IIM classroom learning, career counseling, a soft skills development session and much more. Recognizing the need for early career guidance, Shiksha has also planned a workshop for school students to help these children make informed career choices. The participants of the workshop would also be exposed to the history of Indian businesses through a visit to the country’s first ever Business Museum set up in IIM Kozhikode. In addition, the participants will also have an opportunity of witnessing the festive spirit of Backwaters and being a part of various activities and on-going events with exciting prizes to be won.
Please download the concept document for details regarding registration.
